What is Trinity Innovation Hub

The Innovation Hub provides a space for entrepreneurs, start-ups and innovation teams from large corporations. It will be the venue for organized regular events to bring together academics, start-ups, the local community and the enterprise community, animating and programming the Grand Canal Innovation District.
